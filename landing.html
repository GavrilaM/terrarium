Core Systems

A Living World in Your Browser

🧬

Genetic DNA System

Every creature carries a unique DNA strand that determines its traits — size, speed, color, diet, and behavior. Offspring inherit and mutate genes from both parents.

⚔️

Size-Based Combat

Predators must weigh their options. Small prey can be devoured instantly. Equal-sized foes must be fought. Larger targets? Better think twice.

🧪

18 Unique Mutations

Species-specific mutation trees with visual effects: tails, shells, venom glands, camouflage, pack signals, echolocation, and more. Each mutation changes both stats and appearance.

📊

HP, Energy & Fullness

Three-bar survival system inspired by Spore. Creatures need to balance eating, resting, and fighting. No infinite eating — fullness caps prevent ecosystem collapse.

🌍

Dynamic Ecosystem

Bloom events spawn food surges. Toxic zones challenge survival. Ocean currents push creatures around. The ecosystem adapts and balances itself naturally.

🎓

Evolution & Graduation

Creatures evolve through 4 tiers of advancement. The species that reaches Apex sentience can graduate to the next biome — unlocking deeper evolution.

Evolution Tiers

The Path to Sentience

🦠

Primordial TIER 0

Basic organisms. Spawn into the world with raw DNA. Wander, eat, and survive the harsh primordial soup.

🧬

Adapted TIER 1

Survive 500 frames and earn your first mutation slot. Long tails, dash fins, or adaptive guts — the first branching point.

🔬

Evolved TIER 2

Survive 1500 frames and produce 2+ offspring. Unlock powerful mutations: venom, camouflage, mimicry, pack hunting signals.

⭐

Apex Sentience TIER 3

The pinnacle. Survive 3000 frames, produce 5+ offspring, and unlock the rarest mutations. When 5+ Apex creatures exist, the species achieves sentience — ready to graduate.
